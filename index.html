Catch them All!

On your quest to catch them all, you are challenged to a battle. A random CP (Combat Power) is assigned to you at the beginning of the challenge. You will face 4 Gen-One Pokémons in the battle, each with a hidden HP (Hit Points).

By clicking on a Pokémon, their specific HP(Hit points) will be added to the challenger’s total HP points.The HP for each Pokémon is hidden until you click on them.

You win the battle by matching your assigned CP to the total HP and neutralizing your challenger. You lose if the total HP is higher than your CP.

Note: The value changes each time the challenge is attempted.
